# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Auracelle Charlie is a doctoral research instrument operating within an academic and professional governance research context. All contributors, collaborators, and users are expected to engage respectfully and constructively.

## Standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Respectful and constructive communication in issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Welcoming of different perspectives — this instrument is used across diverse professional communities (academic, military, civil society, industry, diplomatic)
- Focus on research integrity and methodological rigour in technical contributions
- Appropriate attribution of ideas and prior work

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Misrepresenting the research instrument's capabilities or findings
- Attempts to circumvent IP protections or extract proprietary framework details

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community spaces associated with this repository.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the PI at the contact details in the README. All reports will be reviewed and responded to appropriately. The PI reserves the right to remove contributors from the project for conduct violations.
